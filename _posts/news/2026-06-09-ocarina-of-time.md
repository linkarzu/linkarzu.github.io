## My History with This Game

I remember being around 12 years old when I played this game, I don't remember
the exact age, but I could have been a bit older.

I didn't have the little thingy you put below the Nintendo 64 controller to save
the game, and I don't remember as well if I turned it off I lost the saved game.
Man, I don't remember shit, so what's the point of this blogpost then?

I think I did have the ability to save, because I used to play it with my
brother.

But I do remember the adventures and the game soundtrack. I remember multiple
times thinking that my game was definitely defective because I couldn't pass a
level for many consecutive days.

There was no internet back then, or maybe there was but I didn't have access to
it. I also didn't have any friends that played it, so I was on my own.

There were a lot of problems at home, so this game allowed me to escape and just
forget about the world for a few hours.

[![image](../../assets/img/imgs/260609-thux-ocarina-of-time.avif){: width="600" }](https://ko-fi.com/linkarzu/goal?g=0){:target="\_blank"}

To this day, I get excited when I hear any of the songs in the soundtrack, and
I've always said to myself: "I'm done playing video games, they don't make me
feel anything anymore, like when I was a kid".

But I've always thought to myself that if Nintendo ever makes a remake of
Ocarina of Time, is one of the few games I'll play again. So the times has come.

We don't have a game console at home, and my daughter is not allowed to play
video games, but for this game I'll do an exception and we'll play it together.
